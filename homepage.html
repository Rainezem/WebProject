A bungalow is a style of house or cottage that is typically either a single story or has a second, half, or partial story built into a sloped roof. Bungalows are typically small in terms of size and square footage and often are distinguished by the presence of dormer windows and verandas.

Affordable bungalow houses typically feature a single-story design, open floor plans, and efficient use of space, making them suitable for families or individuals seeking a compact and cost-effective home. They often incorporate Craftsman architectural elements, like low-pitched roofs and porches. These homes are well-suited for small urban lots or narrow properties.
